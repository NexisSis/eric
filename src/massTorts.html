Mass Torts

Eric Mausner Law, P.A. is affiliated with Dr. C. Mark Whitehead III, M.D., J.D. of The Whitehead Law Firm, L.L.C. located in both Miami, FL and Lafayette, LA where they focus their practice on mass torts and class actions. Mass torts are lawsuits against companies who place dangerous or defective pharmaceuticals, medical devices, or products on the marketplace that injure or kill a large number of people. Mass torts are similar to class actions, but each case has its own measure of damages. These are unique cases that require attorneys with specialized knowledge and experience. Together Eric Mausner Law, P.A. and The Whitehead Law Firm, L.L.C. can assist victims of dangerous pharmaceuticals, defective medical devices, and defective products.
