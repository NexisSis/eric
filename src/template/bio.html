Why Choose Eric Mausner Law

As a former prosecuter and a former Assistant State Attorney at the Miami- Dade State Attorney's office, Eric Mausner has extensive trial work experience. Eric has also represented members of labor unions in railroad lawsuits under the Federal Employer's Liability Act (FELA). Being a 3rd Generation Attorney, standing in the courtroom is natural for Eric. He founded Eric Mausner Law with one goal in mind: to use his expertise to help Catastrophic and Tramatic Injury cases. Having served the community for over a decade, Eric has the experience in knowing how to fight to represent people who have been seriously injured.
