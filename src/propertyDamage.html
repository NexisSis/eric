Property Damage

Eric Mausner Law, P.A., is dedicated to representing individuals and businesses who have suffered property damage due to Hurricane Irma and other storm damage. After suffering storm damage, it is important to immediately retain legal counsel to protect your rights and interests when filing a claim with your insurance company Insurance companies have teams of lawyers and claim agents protecting their economic interests, which may result in the denial of your claim or the underpayment/devaluation of your claim. Eric Mausner Law, P.A., will ensure you are fairly and justly compensated by the insurance company. Additionally, we are ready and equipped with the resources necessary to file a lawsuit on your behalf.

Our firm's goal is to maximize your recovery and protect your economic future. Let us help you understand your case and fight for you and the best possible outcome.
